How does the raffle work?
  • The Raffle Game provides a different way to win prizes, a way that does not require you to be the number 1 ranked lounge or person. Each time you accumulate 25 Kukui Cup points, you receive 1 raffle ticket, which you can allocate into a drawing for one or more of the prizes in the raffle. Your chances of winning that prize correspond to the percentage of tickets allocated to that prize that you contributed. You can change your ticket allocation at any time during a round. The more points you earn, the more tickets you receive, and the higher your chances of winning prizes in the raffle.
