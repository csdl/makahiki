Performing Activities
  • Some of the activites in the Smart Grid Game involve you to watch a video, attend an event, or do certain activities around the dorms. To do an activity, go to the Get Nutz page and find an activity that you would like to perform. After performing an activity, there will be either questions, code inputs, or image uploads that you will have to provide to prove that you have done the activity. After an Admin approves the activity you done, you will get your points!!!
