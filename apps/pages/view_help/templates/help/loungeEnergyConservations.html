Lounge Energy Conservation
  • In a lounge competition, pairs of floors compete as a unit. Each dorm is divided into five groups consisting of floors 3-4, 5-6, 7-8, 9-10, and 11-12. To win in the lounge competition, your lounge must reduce its energy consumption more than other groups. To simplify things, whenever we refer to "floor" in this competition, we really mean "pair of floors".
