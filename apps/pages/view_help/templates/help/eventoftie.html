In The Event of Ties
  • In the event that two or more individuals (or floors) are tied for the most points, then the timestamp associated with the final goal, commitment, or activity associated with the individuals (or floors) will be used to break the tie. In other words, the individual (or floor) who got to their final point total first will be declared the winner. Under the remote possibility that the final timestamps are equal, a coin toss will be used to decide the winner.

    Given that energy consumption is measured down to a fraction of a watt-hour, we do not anticipate that any floor could tie with respect to their consumption. In case that this does occur, a coin toss will be used to decide the winner.
