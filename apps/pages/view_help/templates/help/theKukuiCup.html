What is the Kukui Cup
  • The Kukui Cup residence hall energy competition is a blend of real world and virtual world activities. In the real world, participants attend events, engage in real-world activities like geocaching, scavenger hunts, and artistic/musical events, win prizes, and most importantly, modify their energy-related behaviors. In the virtual world of the Kukui Cup web application, participants earn points, achieve badges, increase their energy literacy through readings and videos, and use social networking mechanisms to engage with friends about the event. Our hope is that this combination of real and virtual activities complement and reinforce each other. The blending of real and virtual worlds has been explored in other "serious games" such as Urgent/Evoke.
